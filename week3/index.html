Background

Given the continuously growing number of Chinese Americans living in San Diego, improving patients' access to Mandarin, Cantonese, and other Chinese language services is a critical step towards ensuring greater health equity. Members of the community were asked about whether these resources are currently available at their local hospital/clinic (or have been in the past), and if so, they were invited to share more about their experience using such translation services.
